About This Calculator

Welcome to the Calculator Web App! This tool is designed to help you perform fast and accurate calculations right from your browser. Whether you're solving basic arithmetic or need a quick solution for more complex equations, this app makes it easy and accessible.

Key Features:

  • Support for 2-number and 3-number expressions
  • Handles addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division
  • Includes calculation history for quick review
  • Clean and simple design optimized for any device

Why It Exists:

This app was created as a fun and useful project to make calculations easier for everyone. It's ideal for students, parents, and anyone who needs a fast calculator without installing anything.
